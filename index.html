Questions
Is ODORSTRIKE safe on skin?

No. ODORSTRIKE is formulated exclusively for fabric. It contains Isopropyl Alcohol (20%) which can irritate skin. Do not spray on skin, face, or eyes. If contact occurs, rinse with water.

Will it stain my clothes?

No. The formula dries clear in under 10 seconds with zero residue. Works on cotton, polyester, denim, and wool. We recommend testing on a small hidden area first for delicate fabrics like silk.

How many uses per bottle?

Approximately 250 sprays per 50ml bottle. At 2–3 sprays per use, that's roughly 80–125 uses — enough for 2–3 months of daily use.

Does it work on dry-clean-only fabric?

We don't recommend it for dry-clean-only items, leather, or suede. The alcohol content could affect these materials. Stick to machine-washable fabrics.

Is the fragrance strong?

Very subtle. The fragrance (1.2%) is designed to be barely noticeable — just enough to signal freshness without announcing itself. It fades within a minute, leaving your clothes smelling neutral.
