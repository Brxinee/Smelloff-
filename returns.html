Policy · Returns

Returns

We stand behind ODORSTRIKE. If it doesn't work for you, we'll take it back.

7-day return window

You can return your order within 7 days of delivery, no questions asked. The product must be at least 80% full and in its original packaging.

Hygiene note: Because ODORSTRIKE is a personal-use product, returns are accepted only if the bottle is intact and the seal has not been tampered with beyond reasonable trial use.

How to initiate a return

  • Email smelloffsupport@gmail.com or WhatsApp +91 93929 74031 with your order ID
  • Mention reason for return (optional but helps us improve)
  • We'll arrange reverse pickup within 2–3 business days
  • Refund is processed within 5–7 business days after we receive and inspect the product

Refund method

  • Prepaid orders: refunded to the original payment method (UPI / card / netbanking)
  • COD orders: refunded via bank transfer — we'll collect account details when you initiate the return

Non-returnable cases

  • Orders returned after 7 days from delivery
  • Products with less than 80% contents remaining
  • Products damaged due to misuse (sprayed on skin, used on leather/suede, etc.)
  • Combo packs where bottles have been opened and used past trial

Damaged or wrong product

If you received a damaged bottle or the wrong variant, contact us within 48 hours with photos. We'll replace it free of charge — you don't need to return the original.
