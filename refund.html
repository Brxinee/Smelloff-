Policy · Refund

Refund

Refunds are processed for valid returns, cancellations, and failed payments. Below are the specific terms.

Eligibility for refund

  • Product returned within 7-day return window (see Returns Policy)
  • Order cancelled before dispatch
  • Damaged or wrong product delivered
  • Package lost in transit and not recoverable
  • Payment deducted but order not confirmed (auto-refund within 5–7 days by payment gateway)

Refund timeline

  • UPI / Cards / Netbanking: 5–7 business days after return is received and inspected
  • COD orders: 5–7 business days via bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS) after we collect your account details
  • Cancellations before dispatch: refund initiated within 24 hours

Refund amount

The full product amount is refunded. Shipping charges (if paid) are non-refundable except in cases of damaged/wrong product or order cancellation before dispatch. COD handling fee is non-refundable once the order has shipped.

Partial refunds

For multi-bottle combo orders where only some bottles are returned, a partial refund is processed for the returned units. Bundle discounts are recalculated — if the remaining order falls below the bundle threshold, the difference is deducted from the refund.

How to track your refund

Once we initiate the refund, you'll receive a confirmation email with the refund reference ID. For payment gateway refunds, the amount appears in your account based on your bank's processing time. If the refund is delayed beyond 10 business days, contact smelloffsupport@gmail.com or WhatsApp +91 93929 74031.

Order cancellation

You can cancel any order before it is dispatched. Once dispatched, the order cannot be cancelled but can be returned as per our Returns Policy.
